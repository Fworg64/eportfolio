Robot Soccer

About the Project

This project consisted of proving the feasibility of a low cost localization and control system where a clear line of site is present.

Involvement

Our team consited of 2 undergraduate Electrical Engineers (Haiming Lou and myself) and one graduate Computer Science Major (Goutham Miryala). Our reasearch constited of a set of small robots which coordinated to play robot soccer. An academic paper is available for download here or may be seen below.
