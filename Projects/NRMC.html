NASA RMC

About the competition

The NASA Robotic Mining Comptetion is a competion put on by NASA where university teams compete to build a fully autonomous mining robot for intergalactic operations. The official rules for the competition may be found here.

Involvement

I was responsible for the Obstacle Avoidance System. This involved 2 semesters of reasearch in computer vision and several device iterations. At first a structered light sensor was developed, but after sufficient optics could not be developed within budget constraints to perform in the lighting conditions that would be present, the solution shifted to an image processing algorithm. An academic writeup is currently in progress.

Media

Currently, the competition is scheduled for May 22. Becuase of the extremely competitive nature of this competition, our team is not publishing any results until after the competiotion. However, many videos and pictures can be expected!
